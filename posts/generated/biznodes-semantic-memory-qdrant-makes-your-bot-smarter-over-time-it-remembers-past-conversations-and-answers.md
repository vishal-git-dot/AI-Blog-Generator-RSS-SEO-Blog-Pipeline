Hey devs, if youve ever been tired of paying monthly subscriptions for AI agents that disappear as soon as your credit card cycle resets or worried about data privacy when sending sensitive client info through public clouds then BizNode is probably the project youre looking for in this era of autonomous operations. Its not just another chatbot wrapper but an entirely decentralized, locally run business operator designed to remember everything youve taught it over time using semantic memory powered by Qdrant and Retrieval Augmented Generation so your bot actually learns from past conversations instead of repeating generic scripts. The beauty here is that BizNode runs entirely on your own machine with zero cloud dependency which means no hidden monthly fees, no SaaS locks out and total control over where data lives. At its core you get a Telegram AI bot that operates 247 for lead capture but the real magic happens under the hood in your local environment powered by Ollama running Qwen3.5 as your private brain while semantic memory is managed via Qdrant RAG so when a prospect asks about pricing or services your bot references actual past interactions rather than hallucinating answers from training data alone. On top of that theres a PostgreSQL CRM built right into the stack to handle contacts and notes plus automated email followups triggered by specific events without you lifting a finger but whats really cool is the selfhealing watchdog feature which monitors the node for crashes or configuration drifts and tries to fix them automatically ensuring uptime matters because downtime costs money. You can manage everything through a web dashboard accessible at localhost7777 where youll see logs metrics and settings all in one place without needing remote administration tools that require trust on external servers.
