# Are AI Tools Worth It for Small Businesses?

## Overview

Workflow fit before feature count Buying an AI tool is easy. Proving that it improves a real workflow is the difficult part. Start with one recurring task, define the result you need, and test the tool against the current process. Then verify plan limits, integrations, support, cancellation terms, and the controls needed to review its output. A practical evaluation path Choose one repeatable task with a measurable outcome. Compare the time, quality, and review effort before and after the trial. Verify pricing and usage limits on the vendor's current pages. Check data handling, access controls, exports, integrations, and support. Keep human review for customer-facing or consequential work.
