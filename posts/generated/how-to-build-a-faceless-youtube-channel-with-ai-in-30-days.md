Going faceless allows you to create content without revealing your identity, which can be liberating. It also reduces the stress of on-camera performance. The income potential is significant because each video you produce can draw in ad revenue and affiliate sales. You can create informational videos that can be monetized via ads, sponsorships, and merchandise sales.
