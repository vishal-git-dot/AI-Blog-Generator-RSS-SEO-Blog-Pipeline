# Introducing OzymorLab: Building the Operating System for Modern Education

## Overview

Most educational software solves one problem. One platform handles assignments. Another manages examinations. Another grades answer sheets. Another generates reports. Another provides analytics. Educational institutions are often forced to combine multiple disconnected tools to manage a single academic workflow. We're building OzymorLab to change that. OzymorLab is an open-source, AI-native education infrastructure platform designed to power the complete examination and assessment lifecycle. Today, our focus is assessment and evaluation. Tomorrow, we aim to support every critical component of educational operations. What OzymorLab Does AI-assisted grading Assignment and homework management Question paper generation Rubric-based evaluation Feedback generation Student performance analytics Academic workflow automation Teacher productivity tools School and institutional reporting Unlike many grading systems that focus only on essays or descriptive answers, OzymorLab is being designed to evaluate: Mathematical solutions Multi-step reasoning Diagrams Graphs Subject-specific answers Structured problem-solving workflows Teachers can evaluate large batches of submissions while maintaining consistency and transparency. Students receive detailed feedback and can interact with AI-powered discussion systems to better understand their mistakes and improve learning outcomes. The Bigger Vision We don't see OzymorLab as an AI grading product. We see it as the operating system connecting: Students Teachers Schools Universities Certification Bodies Academic Authorities through one intelligent educational platform. AI grading is where we begin. Educational infrastructure is where we're headed.
