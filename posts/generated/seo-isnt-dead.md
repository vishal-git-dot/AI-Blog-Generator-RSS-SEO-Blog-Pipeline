# SEO isn't dead ....

## Overview

Why Great Websites Still Don't Rank on Google You can build an amazing product. Fast . Responsive . Beautiful . And still struggle to get visitors from Google. Why? Because SEO is no longer just about keywords. From what we've seen working with digital products, four elements make the biggest difference today: Technical foundations A slow website or poor crawlability can prevent search engines from understanding your pages. Things worth checking: Core Web Vitals Robots.txt XML Sitemap Internal linking Canonical tags Content that answers real questions Search engines have become much better at understanding intent. Instead of writing for algorithms, write for the people who will actually read your content. User experience matters If visitors leave after a few seconds, that's usually a sign that something isn't meeting their expectations. Navigation, readability and page speed all contribute to a better experience. AI is changing discovery More users are asking questions directly to tools like ChatGPT, Gemini or Perplexity. That means visibility now extends beyond traditional search engines. Optimizing content for both search engines and AI assistants is becoming an important part of a modern organic strategy. SEO isn't dead. It's simply becoming more technical and more user-focused than ever. How are you adapting your websites for AI-powered search?
