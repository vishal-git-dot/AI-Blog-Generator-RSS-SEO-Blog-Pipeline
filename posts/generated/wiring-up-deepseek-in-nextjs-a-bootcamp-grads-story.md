Things I Wish I Knew On Day One I'm going to list out the practices that I had to figure out through trial and error, because nobody at bootcamp told me any of this. It's not fancy, but it catches regressions when I switch models. 5. Have a fallback plan. Rate limits are real. When a provider gets slammed, you want your app to gracefully degrade. I keep a secondary model configured so if the primary one starts returning errors, my app just switches over. Users never see the difference.
