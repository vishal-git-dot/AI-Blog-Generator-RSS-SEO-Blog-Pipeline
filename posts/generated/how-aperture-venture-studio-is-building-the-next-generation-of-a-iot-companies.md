# How Aperture Venture Studio Is Building the Next Generation of A IoT Companies

## Overview

While innovation was traditionally seen as purely software-driven, the next level of evolution takes place at the crossroads of Artificial Intelligence and Internet of Things to address actual operational needs. Aperture Venture Studio is a venture creation platform dedicated to developing A IoT ventures in such industries as manufacturing, logistics, healthcare, infrastructure, and industrial operations. Having originated as an in-house project of GAO Group, the studio has transformed into an industrial platform to translate real-world problems into venture-level opportunities. Distinctive feature of Aperture Venture Studio lies in its focus on real-world implementation, real demand and existing infrastructure. Contrary to traditional practice of building everything anew, the studio capitalizes on decades of IoT experience, established industry relationships, and technological foundation to accelerate validation and scaling of ventures. Specific domains of interest for Aperture Venture Studio comprise asset tracking, workforce safety, inventory optimization, access control, predictive intelligence, and industrial automation. As industries increasingly look for innovative approaches to managing their assets, people and operations, Aperture Venture Studio is set to become an engine of next-generation technology companies.
