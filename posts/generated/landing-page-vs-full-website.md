# Landing Page vs Full Website:

## Overview

One question I hear all the time is: "Should we build a landing page or a full website?" The answer depends on your goals. Choose a landing page if you're running ads, promoting a single offer, or want to maximize conversions. Choose a full website if you need SEO, multiple service pages, and long-term brand credibility. For most businesses, the best solution is actually both : a full website for organic growth and dedicated landing pages for marketing campaigns.
