# How a Hair Salon Automated 80% of Their Bookings (Case Study)

## Overview

Marina runs a 4-chair salon in Brooklyn. Last year she was drowning. Instagram DMs at midnight. Missed calls during appointments. Double bookings because the paper calendar didn't sync with the booking link someone shared. "I hired a receptionist for $18/hour. She worked 6 hours a day. That's $2,160/month just to answer 'when are you available?'" Now she pays $47/month for software. Here's what changed. The Problem: Death by Inbox Marina's day before automation: 6:30am: Wake up, check Instagram. 12 new DMs. "Are you available Saturday?" 7:00am: Start replying while making coffee 8:00am: First client arrives. Phone buzzes. Can't answer. 10:30am: Between clients, 8 missed calls. 4 voicemails asking the same question. 12:00pm: Receptionist arrives. Starts catching up on DMs. 6:00pm: Last client leaves. Another 15 DMs. Too tired to reply. 9:00pm: Finally responds. "Sorry, just saw this!" Result: Losing 5-8 potential bookings per week to slow responses. The Solution: 4 Automations 1. Instagram Auto-Response Bot We connected ManyChat to her Instagram. When someone DMs with "book" or "appointment" or "available": Bot: "Hey! Here are my open slots this week: [link to booking page]. Pick what works for you!" 90% of booking requests handled without Marina typing anything. Cost: $15/month (ManyChat Pro) 2. Self-Service Booking Page Square Appointments. Clients see real availability. They pick a slot. It's blocked immediately. No more "let me check" back-and-forth. No more double bookings. Cost: $0 (free tier worked for her volume) 3. Automated Reminders 24 hours before: Text reminder with appointment details 2 hours before: "See you soon!" text No-show rate dropped from 15% to 3%. Cost: Included in Square 4. After-Visit Follow-Up 2 days after appointment: "How's your hair looking? Post a pic and tag us!" 2 weeks later: "Time for a touch-up? Book here: [link]" Rebooking rate increased 40%. Cost: $32/month (Mailchimp automation) The Numbers Before automation: Receptionist: $2,160/month Lost bookings (estimated): $800-1,200/month Marina's admin time: 15 hours/week After automation: Software total: $47/month Lost bookings: Near zero Marina's admin time: 2 hours/week Monthly savings: $2,900+ ROI: 6,100% The Hard Part Setting this up took three weekends. The first weekend was frustrating — Marina isn't technical. The booking system was easy. Square's setup wizard walks you through it. ManyChat took longer. We had to think through every possible message: "hi", "hey", "book", "haircut", "available", "price", "how much". Each one needed a response flow. The email automation was the trickiest. Writing sequences that felt personal, not robotic. But once it was done — it was done. She hasn't touched it in 6 months. What She'd Do Differently "I should've done this two years ago. I was scared of looking impersonal. But nobody thinks the bot is me. They just appreciate getting an answer at 11pm." "I'd also add the AI chatbot sooner. For weird questions the bot can't handle, it just says 'I'll have Marina get back to you.' An AI could actually answer things like 'do you do balayage on dark hair?'" That's the next upgrade. Currently testing Claude for the complex questions. Your Turn If you're running any service business — salon, fitness, coaching, consulting — you're probably doing the same manual work Marina was.
