Understanding LTV to Protect Your Bitcoin The Loan-to-Value (LTV) ratio is the most important number in any Bitcoin loan . LTV dictates how much you can borrow relative to your collateral's market value. If you deposit $5,000 in Bitcoin and borrow $2,500, your LTV is 50%. Your LTV is not fixed—it moves with the market. If Bitcoin drops 30%, your collateral loses value and your LTV rises automatically—even if you have not borrowed more.
