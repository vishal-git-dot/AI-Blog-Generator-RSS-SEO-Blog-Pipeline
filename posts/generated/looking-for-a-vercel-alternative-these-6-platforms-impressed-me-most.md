# Looking for a Vercel Alternative? These 6 Platforms Impressed Me Most

The application also included APIs, a database, background workers, scheduled tasks, and a few AI-powered features. I wanted to see whether there were platforms that could handle the entire application from one place instead of relying on different services for different components. So I spent some time exploring the alternatives. Along the way, I also found this detailed comparison of Vercel alternatives , which introduced a few platforms I hadn't looked at before and reinforced many of the observations I was already making. Why I Needed More Than Vercel and You Might Need Too. The more I worked on the project, the more I realized that deployment wasn't just about getting a frontend online.
