Growth Drivers The increasing frequency of cyberattacks and fraudulent activities is one of the primary factors fueling market expansion. Organizations are investing heavily in advanced biometric solutions to secure sensitive information and reduce identity-related risks. Another significant growth driver is the widespread implementation of multi-factor authentication (MFA). Businesses are combining iris recognition with smart cards, passwords, and mobile authentication to strengthen digital security across cloud platforms, enterprise applications, and online banking systems. Rapid digital banking adoption, increasing online financial transactions, and stringent regulatory requirements are also encouraging organizations to deploy high-security biometric technologies. In addition, operational limitations such as short scanning distances and reduced performance while subjects are moving may affect deployment in certain environments.
