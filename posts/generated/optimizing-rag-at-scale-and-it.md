Key Components of RAG Optimization Chunking : This technique breaks down large datasets into smaller, manageable pieces, allowing for quicker access. Instead of processing entire datasets, systems can retrieve only the relevant chunks. Bayesian Search : Leveraging probabilistic models, Bayesian search algorithms enhance retrieval accuracy while minimizing the time taken to fetch data. They adjust dynamically based on user interactions and historical data, ensuring that the most relevant chunks are prioritized.
