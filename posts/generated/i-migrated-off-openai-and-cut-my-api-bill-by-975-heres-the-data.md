I Migrated Off OpenAI and Cut My API Bill by 97.5%: Here's the Data Three months ago I opened my team's monthly LLM bill and almost choked on my coffee. As a data scientist, my first instinct wasn't to complain about the price — it was to ask whether the correlation between what we were paying and what we were getting actually justified the cost. So I ran the numbers. Then I ran a migration experiment. Then I ran the numbers again. This post is the full notebook — pricing distributions, quality benchmarks, code samples, and the production results from four live services I switched over a weekend.
