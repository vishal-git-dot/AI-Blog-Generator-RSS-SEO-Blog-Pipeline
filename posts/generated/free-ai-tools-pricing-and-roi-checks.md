# Free AI Tools: pricing and ROI checks

## Overview

Why workflow economics matters Free AI tools are not automatically free once a team depends on them. For builders and software teams, the useful question is not whether the AI layer is impressive. It is whether it reduces planning, handoff, review, or maintenance cost inside a real workflow. Practical checklist Test one real task before moving team work into the tool. Verify limits, admin controls, exports, and support rules. Count review and setup time, not only generation time. Keep unsupported claims out of the decision. Key takeaway Use free tools as candidates, not defaults. Test one real workflow, check the limits, and compare the result against one paid alternative before relying on it.
