# Shipped Orbit: A New Tool for Digital Attention Management

## Overview

Today, we're sharing Orbit, a new app built to help manage digital attention. The core idea behind Orbit is to create a more focused environment for work by reducing the constant pull from other open applications and browser tabs. Orbit provides a dedicated space where you can bring together the specific tools and resources needed for a single task. Instead of having many tabs open across different windows, Orbit helps consolidate them into a focused view. This approach is intended to make it easier to stay on task without the frequent context switching that often happens in a less structured digital workspace.
