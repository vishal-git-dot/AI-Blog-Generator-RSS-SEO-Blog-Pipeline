# How to Add Your First Products to an Online Store: A Step-by-Step Guide

## Overview

Adding your first products the wrong way is one of the quietest ways new stores lose sales — blurry photos, missing descriptions, and confusing categories push buyers away before checkout ever comes up. Here's exactly how to do it right, whether you're on WooCommerce or setting up any store for the first time. What you need before you start Clear product title 3-5 high-quality photos (800x800px min, compressed under 200KB) Short description (shows near "Add to Cart" — matters most for conversions) Long description with specs Final price (cost + payment processing fees + 30-40% margin) Simple vs Variable products A simple product has one fixed price, no options. A variable product has choices like size or color, each with its own price and stock count. The 5 mistakes that hurt new stores most Publishing without a category (kills navigation + search filters) One giant description block instead of short + long split Forgetting stock status (leads to orders on out-of-stock items) Uploading uncompressed 5-10MB camera photos Leaving default "Uncategorized" tag (hurts UX and SEO) Full guide with the complete step-by-step setup, pricing formula, and FAQ: 👉 Read the full guide
