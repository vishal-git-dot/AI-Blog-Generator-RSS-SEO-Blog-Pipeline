That frustrating moment is exactly why you need a stock price alert bot. Today, you’ll build a fully functional alert bot that checks stock prices and sends you a notification the moment a stock crosses your threshold—you can run this script on your laptop right now. Why Build Your Own Alert Bot? Most trading platforms offer basic alerts, but they often lack customization or require you to pay for premium features. A Python bot gives you complete control: you can monitor multiple stocks, set different thresholds for each, and choose how you get notified (email, desktop toast, or even Telegram). Plus, it’s a fantastic way to practice working with APIs, loops, and conditional logic in a real-world scenario. The beauty of this project is its simplicity. You don’t need a complex backend or a database. A single Python script using the yfinance library can fetch live data and trigger alerts in seconds.
